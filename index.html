A journey of personal growth and self-discovery

01

Relating Lessons to Current Self

Throughout my journey of understanding the self, I have learned that who I am today is the result of many layers: biological makeup, cultural background, personal experiences, and ongoing development. Each topic we explored in class offered me a lens to understand different parts of myself, and when I reflect on these lessons, I see how deeply they connect to my current identity, behavior, and values.

Perspectives of the Self

Learning different perspectives (philosophical, psychological, and sociological) helped me understand that my sense of self is not fixed but shaped by both internal and external factors. The idea that the self is constructed through experiences resonates with me because I can trace how my thoughts, behaviors, and identity have evolved depending on my environment, responsibilities, and relationships.

Biological Influences

Studying biological influences reminded me that some aspects of who I am (such as my level of energy, temperament, and stress response) are rooted in my physiology. Understanding this helped me become more patient with myself, especially when dealing with pressure. Instead of seeing reactions as flaws, I now recognize that biology plays a role, and this awareness helps me regulate my behavior better.

Body Image

The topic on body image made me reflect on how my physical self affects my confidence and social interactions. I realized that my body image has shifted over time, and although I try to stay confident, societal standards still influence me. This module helped me become more accepting of myself, appreciating my body not only for appearance but for function and health.

Personality

Learning theories on personality helped me understand my consistency in certain traits. For example, people often describe me as caring, dependable, and someone who communicates well (traits that line up with being high in agreeableness and conscientiousness). This topic made me appreciate that while my personality guides my tendencies, I can still grow and adjust when needed.

Identity Development

The module on identity development helped me reflect on how I formed my values and sense of purpose. I realized I am still in an ongoing process of identity exploration, especially as I take on more responsibilities and think about my future. Understanding developmental stages made me more comfortable with uncertainty, knowing it is natural at this age.

Culture and Ethnicity

Culture plays a large role in how I view respect, family, and social behavior. Growing up Filipino taught me the importance of social harmony, caring for others, and maintaining good relationships (traits that showed up in how other people described me). This helped me see how deeply my cultural background shapes my behavior and motivations.

Sexuality

Discussing sexuality helped me understand identity as multidimensional. It also emphasized acceptance (of myself and of others). This topic made me more open-minded and aware of how sexuality connects to self-expression, relationships, and emotional well-being.

Mental Health and Well-Being

This topic reminded me that knowing myself also means knowing how to take care of my emotional and psychological health. I realized that being someone who listens to others means I must also set boundaries and protect my own energy. Mental well-being is now something I consciously try to maintain through rest, self-reflection, and staying connected with supportive people.

02

Summary of Interviews About Me

When I interviewed people about their impressions of me, a consistent pattern appeared in how they saw my personality and presence within our relationships. The people I spoke with described me as someone who genuinely cares about my friends and consistently shows that care through my actions. They emphasized that I am the type of person who listens, understands, and offers support without judgment. For them, I am someone they can approach anytime, whether they need advice, comfort, or simply someone who will hear them out.

Emotional Safety and Trust

Many also mentioned that they feel safe around me (not just physically but emotionally). They shared that my presence makes them feel comfortable expressing themselves openly. This sense of safety comes from the trust I build and the respect I show for their boundaries and feelings. My ability to be responsible and dependable was another trait they highlighted, saying that I am someone they can count on, especially during difficult or stressful situations.

Balance Between Fun and Responsibility

At the same time, they also see the fun side of me. They described me as someone who knows how to enjoy the moment and make experiences enjoyable for others. They appreciate that I can be playful and engaging, but also serious and grounded when the situation calls for it. They pointed out that I know how to balance fun with boundaries (meaning I can enjoy myself without crossing limits or compromising respect for others).

Overall Impression

Overall, the interviews revealed that I am viewed as caring, trustworthy, emotionally safe, fun, and balanced. Hearing their impressions helped me understand how my actions reflect my internal values and how the way I carry myself positively impacts the people around me.

03

My Manifestos

Personal Manifesto

I commit to living a life rooted in care, authenticity, and growth. I believe that who I am is not fixed but constantly evolving through my experiences, relationships, and choices. I am shaped by my Filipino values of respect, harmony, and family, and these guide how I treat others and navigate the world. I will continue to be someone people can trust, lean on, and feel safe around.

"I choose to be present, honest, and kind. I will grow without losing sight of who I am, and I will care for others while protecting my own peace."
  • Be a safe space for others. I will listen without judgment, offer support when needed, and remain dependable in relationships.
  • Balance responsibility with joy. I will take my commitments seriously while still making time for fun, connection, and experiences that bring me happiness.
  • Stay grounded in my values. Respect, empathy, and integrity will guide my decisions, even when it is difficult.
  • Accept imperfection. I will be patient with myself, understanding that growth takes time and mistakes are part of the process.
  • Honor my cultural roots. I will carry forward the lessons my upbringing taught me about community, care, and resilience.
  • Remain open to change. I will embrace uncertainty and see identity exploration as an ongoing journey, not a destination.

Health Manifesto

My health is not just about my body but about my mind, emotions, and overall well-being. I recognize that caring for others requires that I also care for myself. I commit to maintaining balance, setting boundaries, and prioritizing my mental and physical health so that I can show up as my best self in all areas of my life.

"I will protect my energy, nourish my body, and give myself permission to rest. My well-being is not selfish; it is necessary."
  • Set and respect boundaries. I will say no when necessary and protect my time and energy from things that drain me.
  • Prioritize rest and recovery. I will allow myself to rest without guilt, knowing that rest is productive and essential for long-term health.
  • Stay connected to supportive people. I will nurture relationships that uplift me and distance myself from those that harm my peace.
  • Move my body regularly. I will engage in physical activity not as punishment but as a way to honor my body's strength and capability.
  • Practice self-reflection. I will check in with myself regularly, acknowledging my emotions and addressing stress before it becomes overwhelming.
  • Embrace my body with acceptance. I will appreciate my body for its function and health, not just its appearance, and resist comparing myself to unrealistic standards.
  • Seek help when needed. I will not hesitate to reach out for support, whether from friends, family, or professionals, when I need it.
